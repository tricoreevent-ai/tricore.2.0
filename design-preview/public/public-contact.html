FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I register for an event?
You can register for any upcoming event through our Events page. Simply find the event you're interested in, click "View Details," and follow the registration steps. You can also contact us directly via WhatsApp or email for assisted registration.
What sports do you cover?
We organize tournaments across cricket, football, badminton, volleyball, table tennis, and more. Our flagship events center around cricket leagues, but we regularly expand to include new sports based on community demand.
Do you handle corporate events?
Absolutely. Corporate events are one of our three core pillars. We handle everything from team-building sports days and offsite retreats to brand activations and large-scale corporate tournaments. We work closely with HR and leadership teams to deliver tailored experiences.
What's included in the event package?
Our packages typically include venue booking, event logistics, tournament brackets and scheduling, live scoring, referee and umpire coordination, awards and ceremonies, photography/videography, and post-event reporting. Custom packages can be tailored to your specific needs.
How can I become a partner?
We're always looking for venue partners, sponsors, and collaboration opportunities. Reach out via the contact form above or email us directly at hello@tricoreevents.com. We'll schedule a conversation to explore how we can work together.
