OUR JOURNEY

OUR STORY

TriCore Events was born from a simple conviction: every event deserves the same level of intensity, planning, and execution that defines championship-level competition. Founded by a team of sports enthusiasts and corporate event veterans, we set out to bridge the gap between ordinary event management and extraordinary experiences that leave a lasting mark.

What started as a series of local cricket tournaments has grown into a full-spectrum event platform spanning sports, corporate, and community verticals. Today, TriCore operates across multiple cities, backed by strategic venue partnerships, a growing roster of corporate clients, and a community of thousands of participants who trust us to deliver — every single time.
