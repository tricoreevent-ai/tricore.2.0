ABOUT THIS EVENT

PREMIER CRICKET LEAGUE SEASON 4

The Premier Cricket League returns for its fourth electrifying season, bringing together the finest amateur cricket talent from across Bangalore. PCL Season 4 promises six days of intense competition, featuring men's and women's cricket along with badminton and table tennis side events. Whether you're a seasoned player or a first-time competitor, this is your stage to shine under floodlights at the state-of-the-art Spark 7 Sports Arena.

Building on three successful seasons that saw over 200 teams and 2,000 individual participants, Season 4 introduces expanded categories, bigger prizes, and a fully revamped tournament format designed for maximum excitement. Every match is officiated by certified umpires, captured by professional photographers, and broadcast live on our streaming platform. Join us for the biggest sporting event of the season.

RULES & REGULATIONS

FAIR PLAY GUIDELINES

  • 01 All participants must carry a valid government-issued photo ID. Age verification may be conducted for age-restricted categories. No exceptions will be made.
  • 02 Cricket teams must have a minimum of 6 players and a maximum of 11 players registered. Teams with fewer than 6 players at match time will forfeit the game.
  • 03 All equipment (bats, pads, gloves, rackets) must be provided by the participants unless specified otherwise. The organizer will provide match balls and shuttlecocks.
  • 04 The umpire's decision is final in all matters of play. Dissent or aggressive behavior toward officials will result in immediate disqualification and a potential ban from future events.
  • 05 Teams must report to the venue at least 30 minutes before their scheduled match time. Failure to report on time may result in a walkover being awarded to the opposing team.
  • 06 No substitutions are allowed after the match has commenced for individual events. Cricket teams may substitute players between matches with prior approval from the tournament director.
  • 07 Consumption of alcohol, tobacco, or any prohibited substances within the venue premises is strictly forbidden. Violators will be removed from the event without refund.
  • 08 Registration fees are non-refundable once confirmed. In case of event cancellation due to unforeseen circumstances, a full refund or credit toward the next event will be issued at the organizer's discretion.
